What is a Laptop?

A laptop is a portable, all-in-one personal computer with a screen, keyboard, touchpad, and internal components packed into a single, foldable unit, powered by a rechargeable battery for use anywhere, offering desktop-like functionality in a mobile form factor for work, study, and entertainment.
Laptops are used in a variety of settings, such as at work (especially on business trips), in education, for playing games, content creating, web browsing, for personal multimedia, and for general home computer use.
They can be powered using either continuous wall power or a rechargeable battery, and can be folded shut for convenient storage and transportation, making them suitable for mobile use
