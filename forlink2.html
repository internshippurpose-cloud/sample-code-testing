What is Android Phone?

An Android phone is a smartphone or mobile device powered by Google's open-source Android operating system, known for its flexibility, customization, and integration with Google services like Gmail and Maps, running on hardware from various manufacturers like Samsung, Google Pixel, and OnePlus, offering a wide range of devices from budget-friendly to high-end.
Put simply: an Android phone is a smartphone powered by the Android operating system.
It’s fast, flexible, and open, meaning it works beautifully and lets you personalise it in a way that suits your life.
